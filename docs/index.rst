How Do I Log Into My VidgoTV Account?

Logging into your VidgoTV account is a simple and straightforward process. Begin by opening the VidgoTV app on your device or navigating to the official VidgoTV website on a browser. Locate the login button, often found at the top right corner of the homepage. Click on it and enter the email address and password you used when you signed up. After submitting your information, you’ll be taken to your personalized VidgoTV dashboard where you can start streaming your favorite channels.

It is important to have a stable internet connection to avoid any interruptions during the login process. VidgoTV is compatible with a wide range of devices, including smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, and streaming devices like Roku and Amazon Fire TV, so you can log in on the device that suits your viewing preferences.

What To Do If You Forget Your VidgoTV Login Credentials

If you forget your password, don’t worry—VidgoTV has a quick way to recover your login. On the login page, select the “Forgot Password” link. Enter your registered email address, and VidgoTV will send a password reset link to your inbox. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password and regain access to your account.

If you no longer have access to your registered email or face other login issues, contacting VidgoTV customer support can help resolve account recovery.
